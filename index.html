Our Games

Life's a Drag

Put your dragging skills to the test!

Drag to collect! Drag to combine! Drag to uncover! In Life's a Drag you will be able to drag your way through daily scenarios each with their own twist.

Collect items by finding them in the environment and dragging them to the item bank!

Combine two items together to make a brand new one! Sometimes the most unlikely of objects can go together to create something out of this world!

Uncover hidden items with wacky animations accompanying them. Three stars per each stage adds tons of replay value as you try to master each. Four unique level themes with multiple stages and difficulties!

Challenge mode tests your knowledge of the stages by mixing things up even further. Two game modes with over 100 stages between them!

Right Place Right Time

Can you withstand the time-twister machine?

Jump, duck, hang and crouch your way around a myriad of obstacles in the time-twister machine. As a new test subject, you'll be able to explore multiple time periods old and new while collecting Research Points. (RP)

The unique temperature system adds a whole new layer of depth to the gameplay. Be sure to watch your temperature meters; if they fill you'll be in trouble!

Use a variety of items to help you survive in the simulation! Customize a loadout that fits your playstyle.

The longer you survive, the more intense the simulation will become. Leaderboards add a fun, competitive aspect to the game.

Collect coins and bolts during the simulation to buy more items and unlock new test subjects. How long can you survive in this simulation until you're trapped in time?

Cursed Kingdom

Will you be the last one standing?

One day, four adventurers arrived at four corners of a kingdom. At each corner stood a sign that read: “If what you seek is liberation and freedom: you must master your abilities and conquer the kingdom.”

Defeat your opponents and rule the Cursed Kingdom in this digital board game. Navigate the board using movement cards and use support cards to turn the tide in your favor!

Battle your opponents head-on in duels by leveraging your cards strategically against them.

Beware of the spaces you land on though: Some may help you while others may hinder you.

Cursed Kingdom is currently in development. Stay tuned for updates!
